About Us

Bagati Corporation, established in 2002, is a leading supplier of military equipment, helicopter, and aircraft spare parts. Our extensive network of strategic alliances with multinational aviation companies from Europe and the USA enables us to cater to both commercial and military aviation industries. We specialize in providing complete spare parts, along with efficient repair and overhaul services for various aircraft components and engines. As a privately owned company, we prioritize adaptability and responsiveness to meet the unique needs of our customers. Serving major commercial airlines, aircraft operators, maintenance facilities, military users, engineers, and managers, we are committed to delivering swift turnaround times, exceptional customer service, and cost-effective solutions.

Our Vision

We aspire to be a global aviation industry leader by leveraging our vast network of strategic alliances and strong procurement capabilities. Our focus is on delivering technologically superior products and services with excellent after-sales support.
